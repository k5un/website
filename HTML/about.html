About us

LabRomance is a robot developer founded by former researchers at Stanford University and the University of Tokyo. We are a team from quantum physics, computer science, aerodynamics and automobiles. Our mission is to bring everything motions and emotions by the power of robotics.

Executive Team

Kai Yan

CEO and Co-Founder

Kai sold his first piece of code over internet when he was 10 years old. He was a researcher of quantum information at National Institute of Informatics and Stanford University before co-founding LabRomance. He likes Randonneur and finished a 700 km cross country course in 89 hours along the Japan islands in 2010. His life plan is to travel the earth by man-power.

Kang Sun

President and Co-Founder

Kang has lead a joint project of IBM and World Vision when he was in college. Before he co-founded LabRomance, He has been responsible for developing the cloud system for Yatcko Data Solution Inc. He likes traveling and photograph, and he has crossed Australia continet in 2010.
